$$\left\|\nabla x^k\right\| = 0$$ 
$H(x^k)$ is positive semi-definite

## Practical
1. $\left\|\nabla x^k\right\| \le \epsilon \Rightarrow$  doesn't consider scaling
2. $\left\|\nabla x^k\right\| \le \epsilon(1 + |f(x^k)|) \Rightarrow$  considers scaling
3. $\frac{f(x^k) - f(x^{k+1})}{|f(x^k)|} \le \epsilon \Rightarrow$  considers scaling
